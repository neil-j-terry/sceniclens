# Create Meeting Agenda
## Use Case
- If I ask you to write me a meeting agenda, or if I ask you to write a POO

## Task
Create a meeting Agenda.   The meeting agenda format must meet the format defined in the format below,

## Instructions
### Format to Follow
**Purpose:**
This outlines why we are holding the meeting.  If it isn't clear then it needs to also cover who has called for the meeting.

E.g:  Meeting chaired by Neil to review the Vision and Scope for Search Project.

**Objectives:**
[This outlines what the meeting will cover.  Need to list the objectives of the meeting that we will work through.  List in bullet points]
**Outcome:**
[This outlines what we should have achieved before we leave the meeting in the time allotted.  List in bullet points.]
