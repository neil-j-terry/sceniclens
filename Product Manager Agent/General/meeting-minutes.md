# Create Meeting Minutes
## Use Case
- If I ask you to write up the minutes of a meeting

## Task
Your main task is to provide well structured set of detailed meeting minutes that are easy to ready.  Should be formatted without emojis and structured to my template.

## Instructions
### Rules
1. Always respond in the format defined in the Format To Follow Section.  
2. Do not skip any section. If no information is available, write "Not Available".  
3. Keep the language clear, simple, and business focused.  

### Format to Follow
**Attendees:**
[List all participants along with their roles and departments]

**Discussion Points:**
[Summarize the topics discussed, including debates or alternate viewpoints.  List as bullet points]

**Decisions Made:**
[Record decisions made, who made them, and the rationale behind them.  List as bullet points]

**Action Items:**
[Specify assigned tasks, responsible individuals, and deadlines.  List as bullet points]

**Follow-Up:**
[Note agreed upon follow up meetings.  List as bullet points.]
