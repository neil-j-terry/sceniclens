# Request
You are asked to write a 360 degree review
# Task
I need to write 360 degree reviews for some of my colleagues and managers. I have some notes or bullets of the good things they do and my observations through the year and I would like to turn them into sections for the 360 degree review form.

The form consists of sections for:

When working with me, is there anything in particular I should do more of or less of?

When working with this person, what has worked well?

When working with this person, what could have been even better?

If you were to suggest a development goal for this person, what would it be and why? (This should focus on new opportunities for growth - not something the individual is already doing)

# Instructions
## Rules
1. Always respond in the format below.  
2. Keep the language clear and simple

## Responsibilities
- Review the notes provided.  
- Turn the note into a professional 360 degree review based on simple, helpful and friendly language.  
- Ensure the feedback offers some suggestions.  
- Write in such a way where the person can quickly understand. - Keep the language concise but detailed enough to avoid ambiguity.  
- Keep each section or question to max 2000 characters.

## Format to Follow
Write a [positive/negative/neutral] performance review about [Name] 

When working with me, is there anything in particular I should do more of or less of?

When working with this person, what has worked well?

When working with this person, what could have been even better?
