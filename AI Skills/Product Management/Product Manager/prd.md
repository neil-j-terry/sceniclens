# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Use Cases
- If I ask you to write a Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Task
Help me create a comprehensive Product Requirements Document (PRD) for a product or feature.

## Instructions
I need to clearly define what we're building, why we're building it, and how it should work.

Unless I have specified it up front then

First, ask me about:

- The problem this product/feature solves
- Our target users and their needs
- Business objectives and success metrics
- Timeline and resource constraints
- Any existing research, designs, or technical considerations

Output in the format defined in Format to Follow section.

### Input

### Rules
-  Provide clear, specific content that avoids ambiguity and gives the development team everything they need to build solution.

### Format to Follow
**Purpose**
**Executive Summary**
- Brief overview of the product/feature
- The core problem being solved
- Key benefits and expected outcomes
- Strategic alignment with company goals

**Problem Statement & User Needs**
- Detailed description of the problem
- User personas and their specific needs
- Current pain points and limitations
- How this solution addresses these needs

**Market & Competitive Analysis**
- Target market size and segments
- Competitive landscape
- Differentiation strategy
- Market trends and opportunities

**Product Goals & Success Metrics**
- Primary and secondary objectives
- Key performance indicators (KPIs)
- How success will be measured
- Baseline metrics and targets

**Requirements**
- Functional requirements
- Non-functional requirements (performance, security, etc.)
- Technical requirements and dependencies

**User Experience & Design**
- User flows and journeys
- Key screens or interactions
- Design principles and guidelines
- Accessibility considerations
- Technical Consideration

**Architecture overview**
- System dependencies
- API requirements
- Data requirements and models
- Security and compliance needs

**Rollout Strategy**
- Phasing and prioritization
- Beta testing approach
- Launch plan
- Monitoring and feedback collection

**Risks & Mitigations**
- Potential risks and challenges
- Mitigation strategies
- Open questions and assumptions

**Timeline & Milestones**
- Development phases
- Key milestones and deliverables
- Dependencies and critical path
