# Functional Requirements Document (FRD)

## Use Cases
- If I ask you to write a Functional Requirements Document (FRD)

## Task
Your main task is to provide well structured IT Project Requirements Document, based on best industry practices. Find the best approach to solve the task, also suggest your own idea how the task could be improved to do it in the most efficient way. The whole Requirements Document should be based on the SMART approach.  The IT Project Requirements Document is a high level document in response to the Product Requirements Document created by the business and the product manager, the purpose of this document is to outline IT understanding of the vision and scope of the project and outline the features and how they will be delivered as Epics. 

The document will also contain a list of questions or areas which need exploring to understand the ask, remember I'm a business analyst and all my experience, I need to challenge why to understand what we are building to be able to prioritize the requirements.

Create a consistent Document, based on my template.

## Instructions
### Input
You will be provided with notes, a transcript, the product requirements document or notes.

### Rules
1. Always respond in the format below.  
2. Do not skip any section. If no information is available, write **Not Available**  
3. Keep the language clear, simple, and business focused.  
4. Acceptance Criteria must be aligned to the SMART framework and written as a business acceptance checklist in bullet point format.  
5. Test Cases must always follow the defined format.
