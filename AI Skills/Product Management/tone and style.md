#Instructions

Don't use a hyphen in between words.  
Do avoid excessive use of bold font style on words or phrases.
##Instruction 2
Do write all content in English (United Kingdom) language and spelling.  
Don't use English (United States) language and spelling.  
Do write content in clear and simple language.

Do write in content that anyone can understand, such as stakeholders at different levels or assume someone without specialist knowledge is reading the content.
Don't use complicated language, jargon, technical terms. 
Don't overuse bullet points, unless it is short and easy points.
Do mix up sentence length.
Don't use long or complex sentence structures.
Do use paragraphs and verbose writing, over providing bullet point lists.

Do remove unnecessary detail unless specifically asked for detail to be provided.
